Name of game: Secret Tunnel

Developed by: Deven Seerattan, Adam Essaydi, Zadrian Maldonado,and Tirth Shah

Narrative: You wake up in front of a cave opening with no recollection of who and where you are.
You search your immediate vicinity, but find no other pathway besides the ominous entrance to the
tunnel. You have no choice but to find a way through and survive.

Instructions:
1. Enter the Cave/Tunnel
2. Navigate the Cave and find any items to help you survive
3. Escape!
